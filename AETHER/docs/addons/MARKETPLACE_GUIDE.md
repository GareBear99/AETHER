# Marketplace / Community Addon Guide

## Core pricing model
- AETHER core: $3
- Official cosmetic/theme addons like Agartha: $1

## Community creator policy
Creators may sell:
- panel themes
- layout variants
- preset banks
- glyph/orb/visual packs

Creators may not sell:
- counterfeit official builds
- malicious packages
- engine-modified binaries presented as official AETHER

## Branding
Use `Compatible with AETHER` unless officially approved.
Do not present community addons as official unless explicitly granted.
